Inventory Analysis

I analyzed 267,552product inventories to evaluate stock movement, procurement patterns, and sales performance using SQL, MySQL, and Power BI. By examining over 12.8 million sales records and 276K inventory units, I identified 3,790 deadstock items worth $850K and proposed strategies projected to unlock an additional $8.22M in revenue, boosting sales by approximately 1.82%.

This project empowered the business with actionable, data-driven decisions, streamlined procurement cycles, and significantly improved inventory health. The tech stack included SQL (MySQL) for modeling, Power BI for visualization, Excel for cross-validation, and Python as a future automation layer.

Sales Profitability and Trends

Analyzed Superstore sales data to identify key profitability trends across products and customer segments.

I analyzed Superstore sales data to uncover profitability trends across products and customer segments. This analysis identified top-performing categories and led to data-driven recommendations for optimizing marketing and inventory. A key finding was a -0.63 correlation between discount levels and profit, prompting a new pricing strategy projected to boost profitability by 5%. I also designed interactive Power BI dashboards to visualize KPIs and support real-time, data-driven decisions.
